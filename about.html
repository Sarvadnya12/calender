About Us.

Welcome to AI Samachar, your premier destination for the latest news, insights, and developments in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Founded by passionate students from SB Jain College pursuing their BTech degrees in AI & ML, AI Samachar aims to provide comprehensive coverage of the dynamic AI landscape.

Our dedicated team of student journalists and researchers is committed to delivering accurate, insightful, and engaging content that explores the frontiers of AI research, industry applications, and societal implications. Whether you're an AI enthusiast, a professional in the field, or simply curious about the potential of artificial intelligence, AI Samachar has something for you.

Join us on this journey as we delve into the transformative power of AI and ML, sharing stories, analyses, and interviews that showcase the incredible progress and possibilities of this rapidly evolving field.
